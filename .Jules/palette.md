## 2026-04-15 - Add Tooltips to IconButtons\n**Learning:** In backoffice tables, users might not know what bare icons mean. Missing tooltips and aria-labels for IconButtons causes screen readers to read generic or no names. Using Tooltip wrappers improves visibility for sighted users, and `aria-label` improves accessibility.\n**Action:** Whenever using `<IconButton>` components from Material UI, especially with just an icon inside, always include an `aria-label` for screen reader support and consider wrapping in a `<Tooltip>` for visual context.
